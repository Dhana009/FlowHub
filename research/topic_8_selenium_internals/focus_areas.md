# Topic 8: Selenium Internals & Advanced Concepts - Focus Areas

## Where to Focus (Priority-Based)

### CRITICAL Priority (Spend Most Time Here)

1. **Stale Element Exception Handling**
   - Real-world blocker in SPAs
   - 85% of SDET interviews ask this
   - Root cause understanding
   - Handling strategies
   - Most critical topic

2. **XPath Axes & Advanced Locators**
   - Differentiates senior candidates
   - 75% of interviews ask this
   - Complex DOM navigation
   - Locator resilience
   - High interview frequency

3. **Synchronization & Waits**
   - Foundation for reliable tests
   - 90% of interviews ask this
   - Implicit, explicit, fluent waits
   - Race condition handling
   - Most frequently asked

### HIGH Priority (Important but Secondary)

4. **JavaScript Executor Usage & Judgment**
   - Handles edge cases
   - 70% of interviews ask this
   - When to use vs. when not to
   - Execution context understanding
   - Important for advanced scenarios

5. **Browser Capabilities & Configuration**
   - Environment-specific issues
   - 65% of interviews ask this
   - Certificate handling
   - Permission configuration
   - Real-world edge cases

6. **Shadow DOM & Selenium 4 Features**
   - Modern web apps
   - 60% of interviews (2024-2025)
   - Shadow DOM piercing
   - Relative locators
   - BiDi protocol

### MEDIUM Priority (Good to Know)

7. **Event Handling & DOM Interactions**
   - Advanced scenarios
   - 45% of interviews ask this
   - Event bubbling/capturing
   - Click vs JS click
   - Focus/blur handling

8. **Certificate & Notification Handling**
   - Real-world edge cases
   - 40% of interviews ask this
   - Permission prompts
   - SSL certificate handling
   - Less frequent but important

## What Matters Most

1. **Stale Element Exception** - Most critical (85% of interviews)
2. **Synchronization & Waits** - Foundation (90% of interviews)
3. **XPath Axes** - Differentiates candidates (75% of interviews)
4. **JavaScript Executor** - Edge cases (70% of interviews)
5. **Browser Capabilities** - Environment issues (65% of interviews)
6. **Shadow DOM & Selenium 4** - Modern apps (60% of interviews)
